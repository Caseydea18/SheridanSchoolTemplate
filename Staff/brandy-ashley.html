Technology Department

About Brandy

Brandy Ashley is a lifelong resident of Grapevine. She has been with the district for 8 years, but she joined the technology department in the Spring of 2025. When asked what drew her to technology, she said, “I needed a change of pace, and the technology office seemed to be the perfect fit. The staff are friendly and the work environment with my co-workers is very cohesive!” Brandy and Adam Ashley are proud Sheridan High School alumni and high school sweethearts. Together, they have built a loving family that includes two daughters and a son-in-law: Brooke & Blake Roberts and Avana Ashley. Brandy loves to cook and is always eager to try new recipes. She cherishes time with her children—whether it’s a girls shopping trip, family game night or simply sharing a meal around the table. She and Adam also run a small cattle farm and tend to their beehives daily. In her spare time, Brandy enjoys repurposing old furniture, giving it new life and character. Their daughter Brooke, also an SHS graduate, works as a Medical Technologist in the Blood Bank at Baptist Hospital. Their daughter Avana, another SHS alumna, is currently attending the University of Arkansas at Little Rock, where she is majoring in Mass Communications. At home, the Ashleys are kept company by their two very spoiled miniature schnauzers, Simon and Hallie—who are quite certain they rule the roost.
