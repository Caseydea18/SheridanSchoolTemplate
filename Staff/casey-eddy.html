About Casey

Casey Eddy joined the Sheridan School District Technology Department after graduating from Henderson State University with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Casey enjoys solving technology problems, helping staff and students, and continually learning new technologies to improve the district's IT environment.

Outside of work, Casey enjoys spending time outdoors, including fishing, hunting, golfing, and exploring new technology projects. Casey is passionate about providing reliable technology solutions that help teachers and students succeed every day.
