About Terry

Terry Barker has been with the technology department since January of 2014. He was born and raised in Pine Bluff, where he still lives. About his family, Terry shares, “I have been married to Robin since February 1985. We have two sons, Dillon and Dalton. Both boys have degrees from UCA. Dillon and his wife Trenna have a 5 year old son (Tillman) and a 2 year old son (Theo). Dalton, the younger of the two boys, has yet to meet his true love, but Terry says he thinks he likes school teachers. (Hint hint for any single school teachers who may be reading). Terry enjoys golfing in his free time. He adds, “I have certainly enjoyed my time here in the Sheridan School District; I only wish I had come here a little sooner.”
