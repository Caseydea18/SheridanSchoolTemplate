About BJ

BJ Pullman serves as the Technology Coordinator for Sheridan School District, where he oversees the day-to-day operations of the Technology Department. From managing hardware and software to strengthening cybersecurity and improving the district's technology infrastructure, BJ enjoys finding practical solutions that help staff and students succeed. When asked what drew him to technology, he said, "I've always been a bit of a nerd, which definitely helps. There's just something about the logical flow of technology and problem-solving that clicks with me. I enjoy the challenge of finding solutions and making technology work for the people who rely on it every day."

BJ joined Sheridan School District in August 2024 and is passionate about building efficient systems, improving processes, and helping the district leverage technology to better support teaching and learning.

BJ and his wife, Taylor, live in Hensley with their dog, Winston, and are excited to welcome their first child this December. Outside of work, he enjoys playing basketball, fishing, biking, 3D printing, video games, and taking walks with Winston. He also enjoys spending time with family, friends, and his church family whenever he has the opportunity.
