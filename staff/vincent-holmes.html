About Vincent

Vincent Holmes is from Lake Village, but he currently lives in Sherwood. This is his 3rd year with the district. Vincent and his wife of 5 years Jessica have 4 children - Jaden, Wyatt, Jared, Jr (3J), and Noah. He says, “Jaden, my oldest, just had a baby, so now I am a grandfather. Wyatt is 9 and loves to play Nerf War all the time. 3J is into all sports, but he loves playing football and is a wide receiver this year for Sylvan Hills. Noah is 3 years old and loves playing on my cell phone and is just a ball of joy. Lastly, my wife Jessica has 2 masters degrees and has plans on going back to school to get her Doctorate degree. She loves teaching and helping others grow to become a better version of themselves, showing them there is more to life than the situation they grew up in.” Vincent has loved technology for a long time; he shares, “I had my own recording studio and we were having tech problems, so I started trying to figure out how to get things done without paying somebody for it, and that’s when I fell in love with technology.” Vincent’s hobbies include fishing, gaming, and streaming. He loves to make others smile.
